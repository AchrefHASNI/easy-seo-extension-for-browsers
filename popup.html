About This Extension

The SEO Checker extension is a tool designed to help you analyze and improve the SEO performance of your website. It provides insights into key SEO elements such as title length, meta descriptions, headers, images, and links. With instant feedback on content length and structure, it assists in ensuring your site is optimized for search engines.
